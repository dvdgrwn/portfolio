Who are we, and what do we do?

Our core values

One of the main goals of rød mos is to reduce the carbon emissions. By reusing local waste to produce food and fertilizer, the need for more energy-intensive methods is reduced, while offering a greater local variety of healthy, biological, and protein-rich foods.

There are countless empty and old industrial buildings in cities across the world; why should they be left to do nothing? Rød mos is committed to reviving these areas in a sustainable and future-focused way.

We are creating a business model based on the circular economy, because we believe that nothing is worthless, and by economically incentivizing businesses, we can create a self-sufficient production and consumption cycle.
